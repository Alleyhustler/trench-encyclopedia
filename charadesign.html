Character Design

You open a closet door to reveal a collection of figures and trinkets, each designed to look like a unique character.

I have a lot of character designs done over the years, primarily adoptables sold on petsites (Flight Rising and Dappervolk). Designing characters is fun, but the primary reason I do so is to engage in a community and come up with creative, playful ways to sell (or give out) little digital thingies. It's what I miss most about being active in petsite/adoptables/art spaces!
The volume of art I made from 2019-2021 for this purpose is wild to me now, and the fact that that so much is probably lost in some old forum thread or in DMs and never archived anywhere is overwhelming haha. Oops!
This page has a sample of these designs, but most can be found on toyhouse, here. Designs from petsite eventsor stores will be recorded in a future page. Note that all these designs have been purchased already and are not free to use!
