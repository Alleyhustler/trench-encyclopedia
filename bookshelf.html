Comic Recommendations

You find a cozy seat at the bay window and settle in. The side of the seat opens up to reveal a collection of comics.

Yuri: Every friendgroup needs a yuri liker and I am happy to fill that need. For years, a friend and I have maintained a yuri recommendation spreadsheet that we send out to anyone we know IRL who asks for recs. I have been torn about making that list public because it contains our personal reviews and links to scanlations, which is kind of grey. As a compromise, I am going to be listing the entries without recommendations, and only ones that 1) are licensed in English or 2) I have purchased or read a legit copy (example: at the library). (Oh wow after making a list, there are not a lot of licensed ones.... If you know me IRL and want the full list, hit me up).

Webcomics: Webcomics are one of my favorite things! The primary way I discovered webcomics back in the day was through other people's link lists, and I want to make my own! Note that this is not all the webcomics I've ever read, I am prioritizing ongoing or recently completed ones. If I categorized something as "Yuri", I will not repeat it in the webcomic list even if it's also a webcomi (the categorization between yuri and webcomic is also super arbitrary... it depends on which context I saw it in first). I also have a spreadsheet for this that contains personal reviews, so I won't be putting the full one online but if you are an IRL friend feel free to ask!
